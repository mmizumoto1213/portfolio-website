About Matthew Mizumoto
A lifelong learner who believes every bug hides a lesson!
I’m a computer engineer with a strong foundation in both hardware and software, but my true passion lies in software development and problem-solving. I enjoy exploring how things work under the hood from optimizing algorithms to designing scalable systems. When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me rock climbing, refining my strength and focus (both skills that come in handy when debugging stubborn code). I’m always eager to learn, collaborate, and take on new challenges that push my technical and creative boundaries.
